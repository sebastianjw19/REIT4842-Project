Welcome

This site aims to enhance the user experience of Peer Observation at UQ. Building upon existing frameworks such as COPUS, this project aims to prioritise user-centered design to improve the overall experience and reduce the training time of peer observation. This site not only allows you to begin new sessions, but also saves your previous sessions and gives you options to view them in summarized and non-summarized formats. Please note that this site is a work in progress and feel free to leave any suggestions at the contact page. Enjoy!
