About Us

This website is made as part of a REIT4842 thesis project. We aim to improve the peer observation process at UQ by offering a more user-friendly UI that aims to reduce training time and feelings of tediousness, whilst also offering a more online-adaptable platform to conduct peer observation.

Project Abstract

Peer observation of teaching plays a critical role in maintaining a consistent teaching quality across various classroom settings. Whilst current peer observation frameworks have been shown to produce meaningful results for users, they exhibit noticeable limitations in regard to the user experience, often feeling slow and tedious. Furthermore, the sudden emergence of online learning exacerbates these challenges, as current frameworks struggle to smoothly transition to a digital environment. Addressing these gaps, this project aims to develop a web-based tool that enhances the user experience of peer observation by prioritizing user-centered design and leveraging web technology to streamline the process.
